    Introduction

    You find yourself at a dully lit delivery depot, standing in front of a glass pane. The other side looks pitch black. Suddenly, you hear a voice speak up from behind the glass. Depot worker: Hey, hey you..new delivery guy. The left glass pane side door is lifted for hands to appear in the dim light, holding a package. Depot worker: Here’s your parcel you have to deliver to uhh..to an abandoned gas station. We don’t know what’s inside the parcel or who it’s for but more so...we don’t care. You take the package from their hands, to watch the hands slowly retrieve back into the darkness and door shut. Depot worker: However, the sender who left the package marked it as highly important and wants the package delivered in 5 days. It also requires you to sign this contract. Depot worker: Please read it carefully. There are more instructions in it on where to bring it to which you’d find beneficial. The worker at the depot, who you could vaguely see through the glass pane, next hands you a flimsy folded up paper through a space under the glass pane.

    Contract

    Please deliver this parcel to an abandoned gas station a distance North of the depot. It has a falling apart pole sign with the name ‘Pump & Munch’ but some letters have fallen out. You’ll know it when you see it. A guy with a red trapper hat will be waiting for you. You must hand the package to him. To this contract you agree that: 1. Handle the package with utmost care. 2. Don’t give the package to ANYONE but a guy with the a trapper hat. 3. Don’t arrive LATE or I'll come personally find you; and you wouldn’t want that. 4. And Lastly, DO NOT in ANY circumstance open the package. Please sign here that you have read this agreement:


    Not ominous and vague at all. You hand back the paper through the slot. Depot worker: Thanks. Safe journey.. You nod, and as you begin to turn around, the voice calls out. Depot worker: Wait, also. You turn back to see something placed on the desk near the front of the glass pane. Depot worker: Take this for your travels. You walk up towards the object. It’s a revolver... You walk up the stairs to the exit of the depot and are greeted with light blaring in your eyes. It’s been a while since you’ve seen sunlight or even been up to the surface at all. You walk for a while following North.
