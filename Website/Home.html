Story

The story of our game involves a courier (you!), attempting to deliver a package unknown to you to a destination. All you know is that it is crucial the package is delivered within 5 days. You will encounter other travellers, survivors, raiders and more while you are on your journey.

Each time you play the game, the package will be random, adding a new sense of purpose every time you play the game. You have the option to open the package but you are discouraged against doing so by your boss, opening the package may lead to unforeseen consequeneces.

Gameplay

Playing the game involves choosing one of few option presented to you during each situation. Some choices may be between life and death so it is important to think quickly. In total, you have 5 minutes to travel to your destination and deliver the package, think you can do it?

There is a karma system, doing bad things removes karma and doing good things adds karma. You may find items across your journey which will help you in tough situations.

There are a variety of minigames as well such as keypad hacking, shooting enemies, searching in the dark and more!



Game Themes

You can change the theme of the game with the use of the drop down buttons on the game page.

These themes do not change the gameplay in any way, they are purely cosmetic. Some themes include: Dark Mode, Original, Journal, and Sunset.

Game Accessiblity

You can change the font size of the game using the buttons on the game page.

Increased font size makes it easier to read the text. If the font size is too big then you can decrease it using the other button, allowing you to have more text on the screen.

Game Compatability

The game is compatable on both desktop and mobile devices, feel free to take the journey with you on your phone, tablet, laptop or Nintendo 3DS.

There are many different endings so you can play the game many times and end up experiencing a differnet ending each time, don't be afraid to try the game on different devices!
