Introduction

Welcome to Deliver or Die! Here's how to play:

Controls

During each scene you will have several options, you can either type out the full option or just enter the number coresponding to the option.

Gameplay

Reading the text is important since there is information about what is currently happening in the game. There are also minigame which happen during certain scenes.

Timer

The timer is constantly ticking down, you are working against the clock to deliver the package. You have 5 minutes
